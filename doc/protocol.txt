The mrfusion SPI Protocol
-------------------------

The protocol consists of two layers:

1.) A low-level SPI communication with the SAM7x being the slave and the
    IGEP board being the master.
    
2.) High-level commands that control the slave from the master



Commands
--------

  Floppy select
  
    e           select floppy
    d           disable floppy
    
  Motor control:
  
    o           motor on
    f           motor off
    s           seek to track zero
    +           step to next track
    -           step to previous track
    z           is track zero?
    
  Side select:
  
    t           top side
    b           bottom side
  
  Misc:    
    
    x           leave program
